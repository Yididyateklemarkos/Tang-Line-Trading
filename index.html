What we do

Services we provide

Each division is run with its own dedicated focus — no scattered product catalog.

01

Import

We import vehicles directly from China, including a growing range of electric vehicles, working straight with manufacturers at the source. That means competitive pricing without layers of markup, and genuine selection — if it's being made in China, we can bring it in.

With a team on the ground in China and a team here in Ethiopia, every import moves with speed and accountability from factory to delivery. And every part of the process is fully legal and properly documented, so you're never left guessing about the paperwork behind your vehicle.

Order Now
02

Distribution

If you're an importer or manufacturer sitting on stock, we sell it for you — across the country, not just in one city. Your goods are handled with the same care and accountability we'd expect for our own.

Because we work on a wholesale basis rather than holding inventory ourselves, there's no upfront risk or storage burden on either side. It's market reach you'd otherwise have to build for yourself, without the overhead of building it.

Book This Service
03

Logistics & Shipping

We move freight from China to Ethiopia, then take it the rest of the way ourselves — with our own trucking network handling distribution once goods land.

One point of contact, one accountable team, covering the full route instead of handing your shipment off between disconnected providers. That's what reliability actually looks like in freight: nothing gets lost between the parts of the journey nobody else is watching.

Use Our Services
04

Export

We connect Ethiopian exporters — in coffee, agricultural products, and textiles and leather — with the buyers who want them, and connect buyers with the materials and machinery they're looking for on the Ethiopian side.
